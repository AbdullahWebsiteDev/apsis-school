Courses We Offer

APSIS offers a broad academic pathway that nurtures curiosity, confidence, and competence—from foundational learning to advanced specialization.

Intermediate

Grades XI–XII — Focused streams in Pre-Engineering, Pre-Medical, and Computer Science. Students gain practical lab experience, college-readiness training, and personalized counseling for board and entry exams.

O / A Levels

Cambridge Assessment Curriculum — Offering a wide range of CAIE subjects with modern labs, project-based learning, and dedicated exam preparation. Our O & A Level students develop critical thinking and global communication skills.

Early Years & Primary

Play-Based Learning Environment — Designed to build strong foundations in literacy, numeracy, and emotional intelligence. Creativity, reading culture, and moral development are at the heart of every classroom.

Our Facilities

APSIS is committed to providing a modern, safe, and inspiring environment where students can learn, explore, and grow beyond the classroom.

Modern Learning Library

A spacious and technology-integrated library that encourages research, reading, and self-study. Students have access to academic journals, e-books, and collaborative learning corners for group projects.

Sports & Fitness Grounds

Our multi-sport playgrounds promote teamwork, fitness, and sportsmanship. Facilities include basketball and football courts, indoor fitness areas, and athletics training zones.

Healthy Cafeteria

A clean, student-friendly cafeteria offering balanced and nutritious meals prepared under hygienic conditions. It’s a vibrant place for students to relax, socialize, and recharge during the day.
