How to Apply

Follow these simple steps to begin your journey at APSIS University.

Step 1: Fill Out the Application

Complete our online application form with your personal information and educational background.

Step 2: Submit Documents

Upload the required documents such as transcripts, proof of identity, and recommendation letters.

Step 3: Interview and Assessment

Depending on the course, you may need to attend an interview or assessment as part of the selection process.

Step 4: Acceptance and Confirmation

If accepted, you will receive an offer letter and instructions on how to confirm your place at APSIS.
